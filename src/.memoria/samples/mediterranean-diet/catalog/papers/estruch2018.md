# What it is

A randomized controlled trial in about 7,447 high-cardiovascular-risk adults that found roughly 30% fewer major cardiovascular events on a Mediterranean diet supplemented with extra-virgin olive oil or nuts than on a control diet; it is the republished analysis after randomization deviations were found in the 2013 trial.
