# Claim

PREDIMED's original 2013 report was retracted because some sites did not randomize individually; the 2018 re-analysis accounting for those deviations left the main cardiovascular finding essentially unchanged.

# Evidence

- The 2018 paper re-analyzed the trial after the randomization problems came to light and reported that the primary result held [estruch2018].

# Connections

A reminder that even trial evidence has seams. It `supports` [[observational-associations-confounded|Observational Mediterranean-diet benefits may be confounded by healthy lifestyle]] in spirit — both are reasons to hold the causal claim with appropriate, not absolute, confidence.
