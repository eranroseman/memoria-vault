# Claim

The cardiovascular benefit of the Mediterranean diet in PREDIMED tracked with the supplemented fats — extra-virgin olive oil and mixed nuts — rather than with calorie restriction.

# Evidence

- Both Mediterranean arms in PREDIMED were supplemented (one with extra-virgin olive oil, one with nuts) and were not calorie-restricted, yet both beat the control diet [estruch2018].
