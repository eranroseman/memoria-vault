# Claim

PREDIMED's original 2013 report was retracted after an audit found that some sites had not assigned participants by proper individual randomization.

# Evidence

- The 2013 paper was the original PREDIMED report and was retracted in 2018 over randomization deviations at some study sites, then republished as the re-analyzed estruch2018 [estruch2013].

# Connections

This is the factual record behind the methodological caution: it `supports` [[predimed-randomization-caveat]]. The retraction concerns *how* the trial was run, not whether the result replicated — the re-analysis left the primary finding essentially unchanged.
