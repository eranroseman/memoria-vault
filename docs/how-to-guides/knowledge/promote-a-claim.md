# Advance a claim to evergreen

This alpha.10 guide is retired in alpha.11. The reset removes the `claim` type
and `maturity` ladder. Claims now live as optional `claim_text` on `note`
Concepts under `knowledge/notes/`; confidence comes from evidence, checked
status, and typed links, not an `evergreen` field.

Use [Link checked notes](link-related-claims.md) to record support or
contradiction, and [Build a hub](build-a-hub.md) when a cluster needs a
navigational home.
