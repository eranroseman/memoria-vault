# Weekly review

The weekly review is the structural-health pass that keeps slow drift from
becoming daily noise. It gathers the Inbox, Maintenance, new content, and claim
state into one deliberate review rhythm. The step-by-step ritual lives in
[Run the weekly review](../../../how-to-guides/inbox/run-the-weekly-review.md).

## How it fits the dashboard model

Daily surfaces answer "what needs attention now?" Weekly review answers "what has
aged, drifted, or accumulated since the last pass?" That is why it sits beside
Drift watch and Loose ends rather than inside the daily Inbox.

Empty sections are success. A healthy week leaves little to review because work
has already been triaged, archived, promoted, or routed.
